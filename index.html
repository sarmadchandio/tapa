8 · Observations

  • Pitch rose ~8 Hz from 2020→2024 (152→160 median). Small but consistent. Could reflect aging, stress, or speaking style shift.
  • 2024 Harris debate had the most open vowels — F1 is highest across nearly every vowel. Could indicate more emphatic articulation or louder speech in a more adversarial debate.
  • 2024 Biden debate is an outlier on F2 — systematically higher (fronted). This may be a recording/room acoustic artifact or a different speaking style in a less combative format.
  • 2020 debates cluster together — more internally consistent than either 2024 debate. Same year, same opponent, similar setting.
  • Vowel triangle shape is stable — the relative positions (i high-front, ɑ low-back, u high-back) are preserved across all 4 debates. It's the same speaker.
  • Caveat: CMUdict proportional timing adds noise. MFA alignment would tighten all clusters and likely sharpen the cross-debate differences.
